Canal Sim

Relax at the tiller. Steer a narrowboat through bends, locks and quiet canals.

About the game

Cast off and take the tiller of a traditional narrowboat in Canal Sim — a relaxed, top-down boat-handling game about patience, finesse and the gentle pace of the waterways.

Narrowboats are long, heavy and slow to answer the helm. There are no brakes on the water. This is a game about reading the canal ahead, easing off in good time, and bringing her alongside as softly as a feather. Slow is smooth.

Features

Steer with two thumbs

A throttle in one hand, a tiller in the other. Simple to learn — a lifetime to master.

Learn the ropes

Start at Helmsman School, where each new idea is introduced one gentle step at a time.

Real canal craft

Work uphill and downhill locks, single locks and whole flights, narrow and wide.

Moor up neatly

Ease alongside the wharf, slow and straight, and hold her there to tie up.

Share the cut

Pass oncoming boats politely on the bends, and mind the moored boats along the bank.

A fleet of boats

Pick from a range of boats, each with its own size and handling on the water.

Earn your stars

Finish cleanly for bonus stars: no bumps, tighter times, no reversing.

Cruise at your pace

Pause to plan, speed up the quiet stretches, zoom in for delicate manoeuvres.

Brought to life

A full soundtrack, characterful engine notes, and a cast of voice-acted characters.

Private & offline

Single-player and fully offline. No internet needed, no ads, no in-app purchases, no data collection.

Canal Sim is completely offline and single-player. No internet connection is needed to play. There are no adverts, no in-app purchases, and no data collection of any kind — just you, your boat and the open cut.
