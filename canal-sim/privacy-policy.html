Privacy Policy for Canal Sim

Last updated: 29 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Canal Sim ("the App", "the Game", "we", "us") handles information. The App is created and published by King Paramount ("the Developer").

In short: Canal Sim does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The App works entirely offline on your device, and nothing you do in the Game is sent to us or to anyone else.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at ccwakely@gmail.com.

1. Information we do NOT collect

Canal Sim is a single-player, offline game. We want to be completely clear about what we do not do:

  • We do not collect personal information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or postal address).
  • We do not collect or request location data.
  • We do not use any analytics, tracking, advertising, or crash-reporting services (there is no Google Analytics, Firebase, AdMob, Facebook SDK, Sentry, or similar code in the App).
  • We do not create user accounts or require you to sign in.
  • We do not show advertisements.
  • We do not offer in-app purchases or process payments.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, files, or calendar.
  • We do not record audio or use your microphone (see Section 4 below for an important note about device permissions).
  • We do not transmit any data off your device.

2. Information stored on your device

The App saves a small amount of game data locally on your device only, using your device's standard app storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the Developer. It includes:

  • Game progress — which levels and stages you have unlocked or completed.
  • Bonus stars — the bonus-star ratings you have earned on each level.
  • Lives — your remaining lives counter.
  • Settings — your audio/volume preferences and whether you have already seen certain in-game tutorial messages and character dialogue.

This information is used solely to let you continue your game where you left off. It is removed when you uninstall the App or clear the App's data through your device settings. The App also includes a "reset progress" function that erases this saved data on demand.

3. Internet connection and external links

The Game itself does not require an internet connection to play, and it does not send or receive any data during play.

The App's "About" screen contains two informational links to external websites operated by third parties:

  • The Developer's YouTube channel (youtube.com/@KingParamount)
  • The Chameleons' website (thechameleons.co.uk), credited for voice acting.

If you choose to tap one of these links, your device's web browser will open the relevant website. Those websites are controlled by third parties and are governed by their own privacy policies, not this one. We do not control and are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any website you visit.

4. Device permissions

Depending on the underlying frameworks used to build the App, your device's operating system may list audio-related permissions for Canal Sim (for example, "Microphone" / RECORD_AUDIO and "Modify audio settings" on Android). These can appear because the App's audio playback library declares them by default.

Canal Sim does not record audio and does not use your microphone. It only plays sounds (music, engine noise, and pre-recorded voice acting) through your device's speakers. No audio is captured, stored, or transmitted.

5. Children's privacy

Canal Sim is suitable for a general audience of all ages. It is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13, and it does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children. Because the App collects no personal data at all, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (in line with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, "COPPA") or from children under 16 (in line with the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation, "GDPR"). If you believe a child has somehow provided us with personal information, please contact us — although, as described above, the App provides no mechanism for any such information to reach us.

6. Data security

Because Canal Sim does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no personal data held by the Developer that could be lost, stolen, or misused. The small amount of game-progress data described in Section 2 is stored locally on your own device and is protected by your device's own security measures.

7. Your rights

Since we do not collect or hold any personal data about you, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. You remain in full control of the locally stored game data: you can erase it at any time by using the in-game reset function, by clearing the App's data in your device settings, or by uninstalling the App.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect changes in the App or in legal requirements. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this document and post the new version at the same location where this policy is published. Significant changes may also be noted in the App's release notes. Your continued use of the App after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your privacy while using Canal Sim, please contact:

  • Developer: King Paramount
  • Email: ccwakely@gmail.com
This Privacy Policy applies to the Canal Sim mobile application distributed through the Google Play Store and, where applicable, the Apple App Store.
