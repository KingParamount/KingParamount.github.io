Publisher: King Paramount Canal Adventures

Overview

Canal Cruiser Log is a canal boat journey tracker. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and with whom it is shared.

The short version: almost all your data stays on your device. We do not operate servers, do not require an account, and do not sell your data to anyone.

1. Data We Collect and How It Is Stored

GPS Location

When you tap Log Position, the app reads your device's GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude, and accuracy in metres). This data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database (cruising-log.db) inside the app's private storage. It is never transmitted to King Paramount Canal Adventures or any advertising network.

Photographs

If you choose to attach a photo to a log entry, the image is stored locally on your device in the app's private documents folder. Photos are never uploaded to any server operated by King Paramount Canal Adventures.

Notes

Any text notes you type are stored locally on your device in the same SQLite database. They are never transmitted.

Boat Details

The boat name and Canal & River Trust (CRT) licence number you enter are stored locally on your device. They are never transmitted to King Paramount Canal Adventures.

App Settings

Your notification preferences and active boat selection are stored using your device's local key-value store (AsyncStorage). They are never transmitted.

2. Third-Party Services

Canal Cruiser Log communicates with two external services. Neither is operated by King Paramount Canal Adventures.

Canal & River Trust (CRT) ArcGIS API

When you log a position, the app sends your GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude) to the Canal & River Trust's publicly available ArcGIS FeatureServer in order to look up the name of the canal or waterway you are on. This lookup happens automatically in the background after a log entry is saved, and can also be triggered manually from the log detail screen.

  • Data sent: Latitude and longitude only (no boat name, photos, or notes)
  • Who receives it: Canal & River Trust / Esri ArcGIS
  • CRT Privacy Policy: https://canalrivertrust.org.uk/privacy-policy

You can use the app without the CRT lookup working — if it fails, you can log positions normally and the waterway name will simply not be shown.

OpenFreeMap (Map Tiles)

The app displays maps using tiles provided by OpenFreeMap. When you view a map screen or export a PDF, your device fetches tile images from OpenFreeMap's servers. These requests include standard network information such as your device's IP address and the tile coordinates being viewed, but do not include any data from your log entries.

  • Data sent: Tile coordinates and standard HTTP request headers (including IP address)
  • Who receives it: OpenFreeMap / Protomaps
  • OpenFreeMap Privacy Policy: https://openfreemap.org

3. Advertising

The free version of Canal Cruiser Log is supported by advertisements served by Google AdMob, Google's mobile advertising network. The premium (ad-free) upgrade does not initialise the advertising SDK and contains no advertising.

When the app shows an advertisement, the Google AdMob SDK may collect:

  • Your advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID or Apple IDFA, depending on your device)
  • Your IP address, which Google uses to determine your approximate location for ad delivery
  • Standard device information (device model, operating system version, language)
  • Ad interaction data (which ads were shown and whether they were clicked) used for frequency capping, reporting, and fraud prevention

The app requests non-personalised ads by default. This means ads are not targeted using your individual browsing history or behavioural profile.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the app will show you a consent message on first launch (powered by Google's User Messaging Platform) asking whether you accept the use of advertising identifiers for ad delivery. Outside these regions, no consent dialog is shown.

You can withdraw or change your consent at any time by adjusting the advertising identifier settings on your device (under your device's privacy or advertising settings), or by reinstalling the app to be re-prompted.

For more information on how Google uses data collected through AdMob, see:

  • Google's Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

4. Permissions

The app requests the following device permissions:

PermissionWhy it is needed
Precise LocationTo record your boat's GPS position when you tap Log Position
CameraTo take a photo to attach to a log entry
Photo LibraryTo choose an existing photo to attach to a log entry

5. Data Retention and Deletion

All data (log entries, photos, boat profiles, settings) is deleted automatically when you uninstall the app. You can also delete individual log entries or entire boat profiles (along with all their associated logs) from within the app at any time.

We do not hold any copies of your data.

6. Children

Canal Cruiser Log does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require an account and does not transmit personal data to our servers, so it is suitable for use by all ages.
