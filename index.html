Canal Cruiser Log

Track your canal boat journey — GPS logs, photos, CRT waterway data, PDF export.

About the app

Canal Cruiser Log is a journey tracker built for UK narrowboaters and wide-beam cruisers. Whether you're a liveaboard keeping a compliance record or a leisure boater who wants to remember every beautiful mooring, it makes it effortless to document where your boat has been.

The app stores everything locally on your device — no account required, no servers to trust, no data shared.

Features

GPS Position Logging

Tap once to capture exact latitude, longitude, and accuracy. Every entry pinned to an interactive map.

Photo Attachment

Attach a photo to any log entry — take a new one or pick from your library.

CRT Waterway Identification

Automatically identifies which Canal & River Trust waterway you're on, using live CRT data.

PDF Export

Export your cruising history as a polished PDF report with embedded maps and photos.

History & Filtering

Browse past logs filtered by today, this week, this month, or a custom date range.

Multiple Boats

Register as many boats as you like. Each one keeps its own independent log history.

14-Day Reminder (Optional)

Get notified if your boat has been in the same CRT location for two weeks — useful for continuous cruising.

Private by Design

All your data is stored on your device. No account, no servers, no tracking.
